About Me

How to play

Controls

Up arrow: Jump
Left arrow: Move left
Right arrow: Move right
[A] : Shoot lightning to the left
[D] : Shoot lightning to the right

Level 1

This is the easiest level! You must collect at least 4 apples while avoiding the snakes.
If the snakes touch you, you lose a life. However you can use lightning shooters to destroy some.
Be careful! Lightning shooters don't work on all snakes..
Once you have collected enough apples, touch the angel and level complete!

Level 2

This is the intermediate level! You must collect at least 5 pieces of bread while avoiding the pigs & rocks.
If the pigs touch you or a rock is touched, you lose a life. However you can use lightning shooters to destroy some pigs.
Be careful! Lightning shooters don't work on all pigs..
There is also a chance to gain an extra life by collecting the candle.
Once you have collected enough bread, touch the angel and level complete!
