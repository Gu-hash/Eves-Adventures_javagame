About Me

Hi, my name is Grace and this is my first static webpage featuring a java game project.
The game is called "Eve's Adventures".
I made this project during my first year of university using a library provided by the university.
The game contains 3 levels each with a different story and is fully complete.
At the beginning of each level, the number of lives is initialised to 3.
Below is a video showing me playing the game. I hope you enjoy watching!

How to play

Controls

Up arrow: Jump
Left arrow: Move left
Right arrow: Move right
[A] : Shoot lightning to the left
[D] : Shoot lightning to the right

Level 1

This is the easiest level! You must collect at least 4 apples while avoiding the snakes.
If the snakes touch you, you lose a life. However you can use lightning shooters to destroy some.
Be careful! Lightning shooters don't work on all snakes..
Once you have collected enough apples, touch the angel and level complete!

Level 2

This is the intermediate level! You must collect at least 5 pieces of bread while avoiding the pigs & rocks.
If the pigs touch you or a rock is touched, you lose a life. However you can use lightning shooters to destroy some pigs.
Be careful! Lightning shooters don't work on all pigs..
There is also a chance to gain an extra life by collecting the candle.
Once you have collected enough bread, touch the angel and level complete!

Level 3

This is the advanced level! You must collect all the chalices while avoiding the lions & fireballs.
If the lions touch you, you lose a life. If the fireballs touch you, you lose 2 lives.
However you can use lightning shooters to destroy some lions.
Be careful! Lightning shooters don't work on all lions..
There is also a chance to gain an extra life by collecting the candle.
Once you have collected all the chalices, touch the angel and level complete!

What's the context?

Level 1: This level is based on the biblical story of Adam and Eve.
The snake and the use of fruit hold much religious significance.

Level 2: This level is based on one of the gospel stories.
In this story Jesus works a miracle and casts out demons into a herd of pigs.

Level 3: This level is based on the biblical story of Daniel & the lion's den.
The lions hold much religious significance while the chalices and fireballs are merely used as religious symbols.
